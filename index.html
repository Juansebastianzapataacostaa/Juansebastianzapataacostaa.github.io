Development of microbial consortia derived from the gut microbiota of Eisenia fetida to accelerate composting processes and improve organic waste valorization. Combines shotgun metagenomics and genome-scale metabolic modeling (CarveMe + COMETS) to reconstruct and simulate community metabolism under composting conditions.
